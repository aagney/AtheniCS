About Us

"AtheniCS: Where Motivation Meets Opportunity."

Our Mission: We at AtheniCS strive to provide affordable, world-class programming instruction to motivated, inquisitive students across America and beyond, while giving talented instructors from across the globe an opportunity to teach equally hardworking learners.


Instructors: Our course instructors are well-equipped with a vast programming skillset and have been teaching in the computer science field for years. Our executive board puts all instructor candidates through an intensive screening process to ensure they are beyond qualified to instruct our students.


Affordability: Regardless of financial stability, we at AtheniCS believe that everyone has the right to a high-quality education. With this in mind, we offer bootcamp-style classes at prices unmatched by other sellers. Our modest starting fee of $150 for fifteen hours of instruction is simply a bargain.
