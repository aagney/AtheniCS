    • Register for Courses

    Please follow these instructions to register yourself or your child for a course:

    1. Send an email to one of our customer service representatives (athenics.edu@gmail.com), and include the student's name and the name of the course you wish to register for.




    2. Kindly wait up to 1-2 business days for your course order to be processed. A representative will get back to you as soon as possible to confirm your order and provide you with payment options. At this moment, we will only be accepting payment via PayPal.




    3. After you complete the payment steps detailed in the email, our representative team will send you the necessary information to join the Zoom video call for your selected course, along with everything that must be installed/configured before your first day of class.
